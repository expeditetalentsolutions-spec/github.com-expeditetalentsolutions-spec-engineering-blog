## Why India for IT Outsourcing?

India's IT outsourcing industry is a $250B+ sector built over 30 years of delivery experience. The structural advantages are real:

- **Talent depth** — 1.5M+ engineering graduates annually
- **English proficiency** — second largest English-speaking population globally
- **Cost efficiency** — 60–70% lower than equivalent US/UK roles
- **Ecosystem maturity** — Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Pune rank in global top-20 tech hubs
- **Process maturity** — 25+ years of US/UK client delivery experience

## Choosing Your Engagement Model

## Security, IP & Compliance

### IP Ownership
Non-negotiable: all code, all IP belongs to you. Your contract must include an explicit IP assignment agreement. Governed by your jurisdiction — not Indian law.

### Data Security
- Require ISO 27001 certification for any vendor handling customer data
- Ensure GDPR/CCPA compliance clauses in your MSA
- Offshore engineers access dev/staging only — never direct production access
- Implement code repository access controls per engineer

### NDAs
Mutual NDA covering: source code, product roadmap, customer data, business strategy, trade secrets. Verified by your legal counsel before signing.

## The 5 Questions That Matter

When evaluating any [IT outsourcing partner in India](https://expeditets.com/india/):

1. **"Can I speak with a current client's engineering lead?"** — Not the CEO. The tech lead.
2. **"What does onboarding to our specific codebase look like?"** — You want a specific process.
3. **"What is your PR review SLA?"** — Anything over 48 hours is a red flag.
4. **"What happens when a dedicated engineer leaves?"** — Knowledge transfer process matters.
5. **"How do you handle disagreements on technical direction?"** — Tests for engineering culture.
