## Before You Start: Three Decisions First

Before you talk to any [offshore software development provider](https://expeditets.com/offshore-software-development.php), lock in answers to these three questions:

**Decision 1: Dedicated team or project-based?**
Dedicated teams compound in value over time. Project-based works only for genuinely fixed scope. For most companies: choose dedicated.

**Decision 2: Which time zone model?**
- Overlap shift (1–10pm IST) → US East Coast
- Standard IST (9am–6pm) → UK / EU
- Follow-the-sun → 24hr support needs

**Decision 3: Staff augmentation or managed team?**
Staff aug = you manage directly. Managed = provider's tech lead manages. Choose based on your bandwidth, not preference.

## Part 1: The Contract Layer

Four documents required before any code is written:

1. **Master Service Agreement (MSA)** — governing contract, liability, termination
2. **Statement of Work (SOW)** — scope, deliverables, payment milestones
3. **NDA** — mutual, governed by your jurisdiction
4. **IP Assignment** — ALL code, ALL IP belongs to you. Non-negotiable.
